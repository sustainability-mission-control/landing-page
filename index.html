What is Sustained?

Sustained is a platform that empowers individuals to participate in sustainability missions within their cities. We connect you with local initiatives, offer rewards for your contributions, and show you the impact of your actions.

Why Choose Sustained?

Community and Marketing

Engage with your community through targeted campaigns and outreach programs. Spread the word and grow the movement.

Research and Development

Support and contribute to innovative sustainability projects. Be part of the change by developing new solutions.

Funding Requests

Access funding for impactful projects. Connect with sponsors and investors who want to make a difference.

Agora - Learn to Earn

Learn about sustainability through tailored materials and educational content. Empower yourself with knowledge. Get Redeemable Points completing Knowledge tests.

Task Management

Stay organized and track your progress. See other project records and history. Our task management tools help you accomplish your sustainability goals.

Impact Tracking

See the difference you're making. Track your personal impact, and contribute to global sustainability goals, by engaging in the community.

What Our Community Says

"I decided to start a community garden in an unused plot near my home, I had no idea how impactful it would become. Within a year, over 50 neighbors were involved, and the garden produced enough fresh vegetables to supply a weekly farmers' market. This small initiative not only reduced the community's carbon footprint by promoting local produce but also became a gathering place that strengthened neighborhood bonds."
Jane
"I am passionate about renewable energy and started a campaign to encourage my neighbors to switch to solar power. Through community meetings and shared resources, over 30 homes installed solar panels within two years. The neighborhood has since cut its energy bills by 40% and was recognized as a model for sustainable living by the local government."
David
"After I noticed how many plastic bags were piling up in our community, I started a simple initiative: a reusable bag swap program at the local market. With just a small donation box and a few volunteers, we distributed over 1,000 reusable bags in six months. The local market reported a 70% decrease in plastic bag usage, and the initiative expanded to other areas, inspiring even more residents to reduce their plastic waste."
Maria
